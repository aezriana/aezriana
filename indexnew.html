Food Hunting Experience

A tasty day out, March 10, 2024

This weekend, I went on a spontaneous food hunting adventure with my closest friends. We visited three different places—each one a big festival and viral food place. From Fantastic Food Fest to Kieda Creape House and Nasi Lemak Burung Hantu, every stop was worth the drive. Can’t wait to plan the next one!
